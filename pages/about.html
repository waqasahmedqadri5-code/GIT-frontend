Our Story

Empowering Pakistan's Next Generation of Tech Leaders

Since 2018, GIT has been on a mission to make world-class technology education accessible to every student in Pakistan.

Our Mission

Technology Education, Democratized

GIT was founded on a simple belief: every young person in Pakistan deserves access to the same quality technology education as students in Silicon Valley.

We combine rigorous academics with real-world industry experience, mentorship from working professionals, and hands-on project work to produce graduates who are truly job-ready.
