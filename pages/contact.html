FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer here? Contact us directly.

How do I enroll in a course at GIT? ▼
Enrolling is simple! Create a free student account on our website by clicking "Sign Up" and selecting "Student". Fill in your details, select your course, and you're enrolled. Our admissions team will contact you within 24 hours with next steps.
Are your courses available online or only on campus? ▼
GIT offers both online and campus learning. Video lectures are available online 24/7 through your student dashboard. Practical lab sessions and exams are held on campus. We also offer fully online options for students outside Karachi.
What certifications will I receive after completing a course? ▼
Upon successful completion, you receive a GIT Certificate of Completion. For specific courses like CCNA, we also prepare you for and facilitate external Cisco certifications. Our certificates are recognized by leading tech employers across Pakistan.
What are the fee structures and payment options? ▼
Fees vary by course duration and type. We offer flexible installment plans — monthly, quarterly, or full payment with a discount. Need-based scholarships are also available. Contact our admissions team at admissions@git.edu.pk for a detailed fee schedule.
Do you offer job placement assistance after graduation? ▼
Absolutely! GIT's career support includes CV reviews, mock interviews, LinkedIn profile optimization, and direct employer connections through our corporate partner network. We maintain a job board exclusively for GIT graduates. Our placement rate is 97% within 6 months of graduation.
Can I apply to teach at GIT? ▼
Yes! We're always looking for industry professionals to join our faculty. Requirements include 5+ years of industry experience in your field, relevant certifications, and a passion for teaching. Create a Teacher account or contact us at faculty@git.edu.pk with your CV.
What is the minimum age requirement to enroll? ▼
Most GIT courses are open to students aged 16 and above. For our advanced diploma programs (ADSE, CCNA), we recommend at least Intermediate (12th grade) education. Some short courses like Graphic Design and SEO have no academic prerequisites — just motivation to learn!
